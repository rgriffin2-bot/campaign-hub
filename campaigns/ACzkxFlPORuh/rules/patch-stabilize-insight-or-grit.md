When you jury-rig emergency repairs under pressure—rerouting power, sealing breaches, reforging spars, reconnecting rigging—roll +Insight (careful, technical work) or +Grit (fast, physical, high-risk work).

10+ — You get things under control. Choose two:

Reduce 1 Ship Pressure.

Fully repair one instance of Minor Damage on a system.

Stabilize one instance of Major Damage.

7–9 — You keep things from getting worse. Choose one from the list above, and the Keeper introduces a complication tied to the repair.

Miss — The repair goes wrong or exposes something worse. The Keeper escalates the situation; mark ship XP.
