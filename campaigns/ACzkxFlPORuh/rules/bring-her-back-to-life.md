Repair ship subsystems, remove ship damage, install upgrades, or clear ship pressure. When you work on the ship during downtime, roll 2d6 and - if you have appropriate facilities, tools, or help - take +1 on this roll.



10+ — You can choose two from the list at cost:

Clear all ship Pressure (1 resource)

Fully repair one subsystem (2 resource)

Install or upgrade one ship system, module, or improvement (cost varies).

7-9 — You can choose one from the list above at cost.

Miss — You can either forgo any repairs/upgrades OR choose one from the list at cost but the Keeper introduced a complication or additional cost.
