Not all trouble arrives in a firefight. The long-term pressures—debts, loyalties, rivalries, unresolved history—linger between missions and return when least convenient. These are Entanglements - short phrases that describe personal obligations or fault lines tied to a crew member’s past. 

Examples:

* An old ally who once sheltered me is in danger.

* A powerful faction has begun building a case against me.

* I owe more than I can ever pay to the wrong people.

* A Breath anomaly or alien presence won’t leave me alone.

* I stole something I shouldn’t have, and its owner is hunting it.

* I’ve been keeping a secret from the crew and it’s putting us all in danger.
