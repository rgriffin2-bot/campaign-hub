Handle obligations, call in favors, or deal with debts, allies, and entanglements. Dependent on a downtime setting where this could make sense. When you connect with allies, settle a debt, leverage a relationship, or push on a personal or faction tie, roll +Presence.

10+ — Choose two:

Gain 1 resource

Secure reliable help or information.

Learn a valuable secret, vulnerability, or point of leverage about a person or faction.

7–9 — Choose one, but you must also agree to a cost, condition, or promise right now.

Miss — You pulled on the wrong thread. You may owe more than before, or someone uses this moment to push their agenda.
