When you suffer Harm, the Keeper will tell you how severe it is based on the fiction and the threat involved. This will typically occur as a result of your pressure track filling up and overloading or as dictated by the fiction of the story.

You record harm as one of the following:

Mild Harm — Painful, distracting, or limiting, but manageable.

Moderate Harm — Serious trouble that meaningfully interferes with what you can do.

Severe Harm — Crippling, overwhelming consequences with significant impacts in the short, medium, and even long-term.
