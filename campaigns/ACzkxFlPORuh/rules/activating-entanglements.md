Entanglements are not always active. They come into play gradually, each becoming a narrative thread the Keeper can weave into missions, downtime, or arcs. Whenever a crew member gains an Advancement level that is tied to an entanglement:

If they have no active Entanglement: The Keeper (with player input) chooses one from their list, or the player selects one to bring forward. That Entanglement becomes active.

If they already have an active Entanglement: The player and Keeper briefly decide whether it should escalate (the situation sharpens or becomes urgent), or resolve (the fiction shows the issue meaningfully closed or transformed).
