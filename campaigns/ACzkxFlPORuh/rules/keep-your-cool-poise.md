When you steady yourself and act despite danger—crossing unstable ground, forcing a failing system, bracing through sudden pressure shifts, holding your nerve in a volatile moment—roll +Poise.

---

**10+ —** You stay focused and act cleanly. You do what you set out to do without introducing new trouble.

**7–9 —** You do it, but something gives. The Keeper introduces a complication: a cost, a hard bargain, a trade-off, or a worse or fragile outcome.

**Miss —** The moment slips out of your control. The Keeper escalates the situation; mark XP.
