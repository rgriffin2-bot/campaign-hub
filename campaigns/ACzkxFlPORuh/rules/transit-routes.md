Travel in Haven is not a single roll. It is a choice about what you are willing to risk in order to get where you’re going.

Moving between planets, stations, cloud systems, or distant habitats sets the initial conditions for the scenes that follow. Routes determine what kind of danger you face, who notices you, how much strain the ship carries, and what you don’t know when you arrive.

You don’t roll to see if you arrive. You choose how you travel—and deal with the consequences of that choice.
