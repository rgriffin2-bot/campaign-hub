When you step in to support another crew member—steadying them on unstable footing, feeding them critical data, bracing against shifting gravity, laying down covering fire—roll +Poise.

---

**10+ —** Your support lands cleanly. Their roll gets +1, and you may clear 1 Pressure.

**7–9 —** Your help works, but it costs you. They get +1; mark 1 Pressure or the Keeper introduces a complication for you.

**Miss —** Your intervention backfires. Mark 1 Pressure and the Keeper escalates the situation; mark XP.
