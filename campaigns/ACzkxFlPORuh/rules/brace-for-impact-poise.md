When the ship is about to take a major hit—weather burst, debris collision, hostile fire, pressure shock—and you try to soften or redirect the blow, roll +Poise.

10+ — Choose two:

Keep the ship on course and under control.

Reduce incoming Ship Pressure by 1.

Choose one system or area; it avoids damage or negative tags from this hit.

Use the force to reposition in a favorable direction; take +1 forward on the next ship move this scene.

7–9 — Choose one from the list above, and the Keeper introduces a complication tied to the hit.

Miss — You fail to mitigate the blow. The ship takes the full impact, and the Keeper escalates the situation; mark ship XP.
