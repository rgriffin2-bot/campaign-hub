In Haven, danger doesn’t end when a single roll is resolved. It accumulates.

Crew members and ships can push through fear, exhaustion, bad calls, and physical damage—but strain builds as they do. This section separates immediate pressure from lasting harm and damage, showing how risk carries forward across a mission.

You can act while strained. You can function while damaged. But every push leaves a mark, and ignoring it only narrows your options. These rules track how consequences build and what happens when things break.
