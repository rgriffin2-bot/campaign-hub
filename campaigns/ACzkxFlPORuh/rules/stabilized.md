Stabilized describes a temporary state where an immediate failure, collapse, or escalation has been halted, but the underlying damage or danger remains. Stabilization prevents an ongoing crisis from getting worse; it does not repair what was broken. Full recovery requires proper repair, treatment, or downtime. Further strain, damage, or severe exertion can quickly undo the stabilization. This term applies equally to crew members (stabilized after being taken out of action) and ships systems (stabilized after major damage).
