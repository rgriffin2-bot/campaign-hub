In appropriate locations, acquire gear, supplies, tools, or equipment. Dependent on a downtime setting where this could make sense. When you try to buy, barter, fabricate, or requisition something, describe what you want and spend 1 resource for standard gear or 2+ resources for specialized or restricted equipment. Then roll +Presence:

10+ — You get it cleanly. You also gain a small bonus: extra ammo, an upgraded variant, or leverage with the seller.

7–9 — You can get it at cost.

Miss — You still get something, but it’s compromised or comes with trouble.

It comes with strings attached.

You owe someone a favor.

It’s slightly compromised (quirky, delicate, temperamental).
