When you deliberately engage with a Maker-, Steward-, or Breath-related system or phenomenon that does not follow human rules or procedures, describe what you’re attempting and roll +Resonance.

This move reflects training, intuition, and hard-earned familiarity with Haven’s non-human systems—not direct control or supernatural command.

---

**10+ —** You succeed. Also, choose one:

* You identify a stable way to interact with it; take +1 forward when acting on this understanding.

* Your success creates breathing room; clear one Pressure from you or an ally

* You gain a clear, actionable understanding of what the phenomenon is doing or about to do.

**7–9 —** You succeed, but at a cost. Mark 1 Pressure, and the Keeper introduces a complication tied to the nature of the phenomenon.

**Miss —** You misjudge or overreach. The phenomenon responds on its own terms, and the Keeper escalates the situation; mark 1 Pressure and mark XP.
