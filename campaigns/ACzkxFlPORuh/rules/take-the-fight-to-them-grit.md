When you confront a dangerous foe—fighting, grappling, forcing compliance—roll +Grit.

---

**10+ —** Trade pressure and choose 1 advantage:

* Deal +1 damage (if your initial attack causes damage).

* Take –1 Pressure.

* Force the foe into a worse position or out of cover.

* Create an opening; you or an ally gains +1 forward.

**7–9 —** You trade pressure with no extra advantage.

**Miss —** You’re hit hard but your move doesn’t connect; mark XP.
