When you take downtime to repair, modify, or upgrade gear, describe what you’re changing. If you have appropriate facilities, tools, or help - take +1 forward on this move.

Roll +Insight when the change depends on calibration, reconfiguration, or understanding how the gear works—interfaces, electronics, software, sensors, or precision mechanisms.

Roll +Grit when the change depends on reinforcement, reshaping, or brute adjustment—armor, weapons, frames, housings, or components that must be forced to hold.

10+ — You can choose two from the list at cost:

Add a positive gear tag or remove a negative gear tag (1 resource)

Remove the damaged condition (2 resource)

Upgrade or modify your gear (cost varies).

7-9 — You can choose one from the list above at cost.

Miss — You can either forgo any repairs/upgrades OR choose one from the list at cost but the Keeper introduced a complication or additional cost.
