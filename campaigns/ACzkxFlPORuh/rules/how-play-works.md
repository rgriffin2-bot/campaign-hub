Play is a conversation.

One player—the Keeper—portrays the world, its dangers, and its responses. Everyone else plays a member of the crew. You describe what you do. The Keeper describes what happens. When your actions carry risk or uncertainty, the rules step in to help determine how things unfold.

There are no rigid turns or exhaustive simulations. Instead, play flows through a repeating loop:

You describe what you do, grounded in the story.

The Keeper clarifies the situation and what’s at stake.

If a move is triggered, you roll and resolve it.

The Keeper responds with the fruits of that roll - success, complications, and escalations.

The fiction updates, and the conversation continues.
