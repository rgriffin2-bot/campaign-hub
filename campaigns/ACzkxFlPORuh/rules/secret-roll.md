A secret roll is a roll made by the Keeper on a player’s behalf. The Keeper knows the result, but the player does not; the outcome is revealed only through the fiction. Secret rolls are used when knowing the result would undermine uncertainty, most often for moves that involve reading a situation or asking questions (e.g. Read the Currents or Size It Up).
