This game separates immediate danger from lasting injury. Crews are hard to kill, but the galaxy keeps finding new ways to mess up their lives. Every crew has:

Pressure is the accumulating pressure, fear, exhaustion, and psychic strain that builds up during a mission.

Harm is the serious physical or psychological injury that puts a crew member down if it goes too far.



## Crew Pressure



Pressure is fear, exhaustion, close calls, minor injuries, psychic backlash, guilt, and all the other ways the work grinds a crew down. You mark 1 Pressure when:

A move result or Keeper move would normally inflict small damage

You push yourself into obvious strain: dangerous EVA, overclocking weird abilities, taking the long way around a threat to keep others safe.



Pressure clears at the end of each arc or after significant downtime in a safe place. Certain moves, abilities, or ship systems may also clear Pressure mid-session.
