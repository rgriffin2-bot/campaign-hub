When you engage ship-mounted weapons, roll +Grit.

10+ — Direct hit. Trade Ship Pressure and choose 1:

Deal an additional Pressure to the target.

Temporarily impair a random system (roll 1d6).

1 — Helm & Control

2 — Engines & Drive

3 — Sensor & Arrays

4 — Hull & Structure

5 — Power & Flow

6 — Weapons & Boarding

Your next attack against this target takes +1 forward.

Keep the target’s focus on your ship

7–9 — You trade ship pressure; no extra advantage.

Miss — The shot doesn’t connect or is otherwise negated. Take ship pressure; mark ship XP.
