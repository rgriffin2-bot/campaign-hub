Every crew member has five core stats. They tell you how you hold yourself in danger, how you read a room or a stormfront, how you push your body past its limits, and how you connect—however faintly—to the deeper forces that shape Haven.

Poise. Your steadiness under fire. Poise measures how well you keep your cool when the deck starts to tilt: quick reactions, calm execution, and the discipline to act cleanly in dangerous or uncertain situations.

Insight. Your sharpness of mind—perception, intuition, analysis. Insight governs how well you spot clues, read situations, parse anomalies, and understand what’s really happening before it hits you.

Grit. Your physical resilience and fighting edge. Grit is toughness, endurance, and the will to put your body between danger and the people or objectives that matter. When you take a hit or deliver one, Grit decides how it goes.

Presence. Your ability to influence others—through charm, authority, empathy, or pressure. Presence covers negotiation, commanding attention, defusing tension, and reading how people might respond to you.

Resonance. Your ability to understand and interact with the strange phenomena that shape Haven. Resonance can reflect intuitive sensitivity or disciplined study of the Makers and Haven: a knack for noticing patterns in Breath currents, interpreting Maker relic logic, or reading distortions in the habitable expanse. A high Resonance doesn’t make you a miracle-worker on its own, but it means you can align with, analyze, or safely handle the unknown more effectively than most.
