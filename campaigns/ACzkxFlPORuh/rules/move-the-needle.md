Advance a long-term project. When you spend time pushing a long-term project forward—building, researching, scheming, forging alliances, crafting, modifying gear, restoring relics—describe your approach and make a roll determined by the Keeper.

10+ — Mark 2 boxes on the project clock.

7–9 — Mark 1 box on the project clock.

Miss — Mark no progress. You may spend a resource to mark 1 on the project clock.
