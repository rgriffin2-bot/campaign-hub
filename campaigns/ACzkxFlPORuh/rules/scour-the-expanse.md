Search for salvage, parts, materials, or rare components. When you comb ports, ruins, scrapyards, markets, back channels, or the atmospheric drift for anything useful, roll +Insight.

10+ — Gain 2 resources and distribute between crew and ship. Alternatively, you may gain 1 resource and find something extra: rare parts, a clue, or a useful lead.

7–9 — Gain 1 resource, but choose one:

You attract the wrong kind of attention.

What you find is unstable, dangerous, or hard to move.

Someone else wants it back.

Miss — You come up empty or into trouble. Mark 1 Pressure.
