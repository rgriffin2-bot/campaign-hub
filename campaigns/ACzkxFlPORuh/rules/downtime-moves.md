Downtime always takes place in a safe, stable environment.

Between missions, travel legs, or major complications, crews take time to breathe, recover, and prepare. Downtime is when you fix the ship, heal what can’t be shaken off in the field, negotiate resources, explore personal entanglements, and make choices that shape the crew’s future.

Each crew member typically gets two downtime actions, though the Keeper may adjust based on fiction or mission pacing.
