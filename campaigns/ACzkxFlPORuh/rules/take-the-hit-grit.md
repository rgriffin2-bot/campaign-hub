When you throw yourself into danger to shield someone — roll +Grit.

---

**10+ —** You protect them completely. Choose 1:

* Reduce the pressure you take by 1

* All impending danger is now focused on you.

* You inflict harm on the enemy

* Create an opening; an ally gains +1 forward.

**7–9 —** You protect them, but you take the full cost of the danger.

**Miss —** You fail and make things worse. The Keeper escalates the situation; mark XP.
