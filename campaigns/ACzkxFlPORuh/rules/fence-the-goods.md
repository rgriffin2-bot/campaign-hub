Unload illicit, restricted, or dangerous items. Dependent on a downtime setting where this could make sense. When you try to sell anything illegal, cursed, explosive, forbidden, or Breath-touched, roll +Presence.

10+ — Gain 2 resources and the buyer keeps quiet.
7–9 — Gain 1 Scrap, but the Keeper chooses one:

The sale draws unwanted attention - from the authorities, rival gangs, or debtors.

The item was too hot - the buyer only agreed if you owe them a favor.

[more items]

Miss — Trouble. Maybe a sting, double-cross, theft attempt, or faction pressure.
