Gear is not a pile of numbers or a checklist of bonuses. It’s a collection of tools, weapons, armor, and systems your crew relies on when things turn dangerous or uncertain.

In Haven, gear exists inside pressure. Storms, violence, scarcity, interference from ancient systems, and simple wear all take their toll. It gets stressed, pushed past design limits, patched back together, and occasionally made better—or worse—by the choices you make.

Most of the time, gear simply does what it’s supposed to do. When the fiction turns risky or consequences matter, the state of your gear comes into focus. That’s when tags are applied, removed, exploited, or upgraded to reflect what’s really going on under the surface.
