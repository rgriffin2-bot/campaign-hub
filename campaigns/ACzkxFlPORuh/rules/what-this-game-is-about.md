This is a game about a crew under pressure.

About hard calls made in unstable situations.
About ships pushed past their limits.
About trust, tension, and what happens when people with different beliefs and histories are forced to rely on one another.

Missions rarely go exactly as planned. Storms shift. Old technology wakes up. Factions interfere. Someone panics at the wrong moment. When things go wrong, the question isn’t “Do you fail?”—it’s “What does it cost, and what do you do next?”

You will:

Navigate dangerous environments and unpredictable weather

Deal with factions, settlements, and powers that don’t share your priorities

Encounter anomalies tied to the Makers, the Stewards, and the Breath

Make choices that trade safety for speed and stability for opportunity

Watch your crew change—sometimes for the better, sometimes for the worse

The rules are built to support this kind of play. They focus on momentum, consequences, and shared decision-making rather than tactical precision.
