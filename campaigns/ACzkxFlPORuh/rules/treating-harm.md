Harm does not disappear on its own. Recovery usually happens through:

Downtime actions (Catch Your Breath)

Specialized gear, facilities, or playbook moves

Narrative turning points

When Harm is treated or other recovery occurs, the Keeper may:

Clear a Harm,

Downgrade a Harm (Severe → Moderate, Moderate → Mild)

Mark it as an Old Wound—still present, but no longer worsening or actively interfering unless pushed.
