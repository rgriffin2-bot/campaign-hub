A **complication** is an immediate problem, cost, or constraint that arises from risky action - typically the result of a mixed success. Complications make the situation harder without resolving it outright, often by adding pressure, limiting options, or introducing a new obstacle that must be dealt with. 

An **escalation** is a significant worsening of the situation that raises the stakes or changes the nature of the conflict - typically the result of miss. Escalations introduce new dangers, intensify existing threats, or push the scene toward crisis.
