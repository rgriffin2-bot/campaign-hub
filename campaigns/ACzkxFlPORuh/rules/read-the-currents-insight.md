When you have time to observe, listen, or reflect—reading people, environments, or systems without immediate pressure— choose an approach and make a secret +Insight roll. The response you get will be based on your approach (e.g. if you use your sense of smell, you can only get an answer that makes sense through smell).

This move is about understanding the situation before it turns dangerous. You’re gathering perspective, noticing patterns, and forming a mental map of what’s really going on. You might be talking with someone at length, watching a system cycle, studying a phenomenon, or quietly assessing a space before acting.

---

**10+ —** Hold 2.

**7–9 —** Hold 1.

**Miss —** Hold 1, but the answer will leave you with an incomplete or skewed understanding. Because this is a secret roll, you won’t know that the information is bad until you’re dealing with the consequences. DO NOT mark XP.
