Pressure represents stress, exhaustion, strain, more minor physical harm, or mounting danger. It builds as you push yourself or take hits. Unless otherwise modified, every crew member has a max pressure gauge of five. When a crew member’s pressure reaches five, they experience Harm and the gauge resets. Otherwise, pressure usually clears through moves or tools.
