When you push the ship beyond safe tolerances—overextending sails, forcing engines, venting ballast, or fighting brutal gravity—roll +Grit.

10+ — The ship surges exactly as intended. You do what you set out to do, and choose one:

Chain the action; another crew member can immediately follow up without added risk.

You carry momentum forward; take +1 forward on the next ship move this scene.

You deny pursuit; the opposition can’t immediately follow or respond.

7–9 — The burn works, but it’s ugly. The Keeper introduces a complication tied to the push.

Miss — The burn goes wrong. The Keeper escalates the situation; mark ship XP.
