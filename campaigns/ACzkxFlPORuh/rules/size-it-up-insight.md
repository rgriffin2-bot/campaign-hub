When you take a split second to assess a volatile, dangerous, or fast-moving situation before acting, there is a secret +Insight roll.

This move is about quick judgment under pressure. You don’t have time to be thorough—only to notice what matters right now before something breaks, explodes, panics, or turns hostile.

---

**10+ —** Hold 2.

**7–9 —** Hold 1.

**Miss —** Hold 1, but the answer will leave you with an incomplete or skewed understanding. Because this is a secret roll, you won’t know that the information is bad until you’re dealing with the consequences. DO NOT mark XP.
