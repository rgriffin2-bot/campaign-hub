Most rolls follow this formula: 2d6 + [character modifier] + [context modifier]

The character modifier comes from the stat listed in the move and the specific modifier on the player’s character sheet

The context modifier reflects how the current situation affects the attempt. If no context modifier applies, treat it as +0.

A 10+ represents a success, 7-9 represents a mixed success, and 6 or less is a miss.
