Misses. Whenever you roll a 6 or less on certain moves, mark 1 XP. It will be explicitly stated in the relevant move text. The goal is to have experience tied to significant failures (e.g. I missed a shot and put myself in real danger) rather than insignificant failures (e.g. my investigative role failed).



End-of-session questions

At the end of each session, answer these as a group. For each “yes,” everyone marks 1 XP:

- Did we learn something new and important about Haven, its forces, or what maintains it?

- Did we learn something new and important about a crew member?

- Did we overcome a significant obstacle or make meaningful progress toward our goals?



Resolving Entanglements.

When an Entanglement is resolved, the player marks 1 XP and that Entanglement is retired. Resolution does not require a tidy or uplifting ending—only that the situation is no longer hanging over the character in the same way.
