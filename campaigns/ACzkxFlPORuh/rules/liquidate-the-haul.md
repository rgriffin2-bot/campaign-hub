Sell salvage, cargo, or basic spoils from the mission.  Dependent on a downtime setting where this could make sense. When you bring your mission haul to market, roll +Presence.

10+ — Choose 2

Gain 2 resources to the ship resource track.

You find a buyer who owes you a future favor.

[more items]

7–9 — Choose 1.

Miss — You get paid 1 resource, but something goes sideways. The Keeper makes a move:
 a stolen crate, a disputed claim, a dangerous buyer, or an owed debt.
