This move is now about reading, surviving, and exploiting hostile movements you don’t control. When the ship commits to a dangerous flow—atmospheric rivers, pressure tunnels, storm bands, Steward wakes, or fast-moving jet streams—roll +Insight (anticipatory reading and prediction) or +Poise (reactive flying under pressure).

10+ — You catch the current cleanly. Choose one:

You gain advantageous position relative to a threat; take +1 forward on a related ship move.

You spot how the current will change; ask the Keeper one question about what’s coming next.

Your passage leaves little trace; avoid immediate attention or pursuit.

7–9 — You stay on course, but the ride is rough. The Keeper introduces a complication tied to the current.

Miss — You lose the current or it turns hostile. The Keeper escalates the situation; mark ship XP.
