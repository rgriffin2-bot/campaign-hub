Clear your head, tend wounds, and recover from the last mission. When you take time during downtime to rest, treat injuries, or otherwise recover, roll 2d6. If you have appropriate facilities, tools, or skilled help, take +1 forward on this roll.

10+ — Choose two options from the list below, without cost. You may choose the same option more than once.

Clear all Pressure from yourself or an ally.

Resolve one Harm affecting you or an ally. Depending on the fiction, this may clear a Harm entirely or convert active Harm into an Old Wound

Gain +1 forward to your next roll using each of two different stats.

7–9 — Choose one option from the list, without cost. You may choose a second option by paying 1 resource.

Miss — You may choose one option by paying 1 resource, and the Keeper may introduce a complication, delay, or lingering concern.
