Luck still represents the universe bending in the crews’ favour. When you spend a point of Luck, choose one: After you roll a move, change the result to a 12. Turn an incoming hit into 0 Harm and 0 Pressure. You still face any non-damage consequences, but the blow doesn’t lay a finger on you. When you would fill your Pressure track and trigger, spend Luck to cancel the trigger and immediately clear all Pressure instead. Luck is only provided at the discretion of the Keeper - typically for exceptional role playing or making dangerous choices that are in line with your character’s motivations.
