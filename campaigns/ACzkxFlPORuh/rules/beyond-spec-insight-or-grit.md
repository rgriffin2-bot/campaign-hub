When you force a human-built system to operate outside its normal limits—overriding safeties, pushing controls beyond spec, or driving machinery or software to do what you want—describe your goal and roll:

* +Insight if this is careful, technical, or methodical

* +Grit if this is rushed, physical, or done under strain

This move represents competent, professional interaction with technology—not covert intrusion or specialized exploitation. (Hacking, sabotage, or bypassing secured systems is handled by playbook moves.)

---

**10+ —** You succeed. Choose one:

* You maintain control or access longer than expected.

* You push the system to produce a stronger or cleaner result than you’d expect.

* You set up a clear opening; take +1 forward for yourself or an ally acting on this.

**7–9 —** You succeed, but it’s unstable. Mark 1 Pressure, and the Keeper introduces a complication tied to the system or environment.

**Miss —** The system refuses, fails, or cascades under the strain. You don’t get the effect, and the Keeper escalates the situation; mark XP.
