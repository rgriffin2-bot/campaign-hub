Most journeys pass in summary. When danger, strain, or attention spikes, the Keeper may frame a travel scene. Common triggers include:

Severe weather or pressure fronts

Patrols, pursuit, interception, or bureaucratic interference

Systems nearing overload

Anomalies reacting to your passage

During a travel scene, use existing moves as normal:

Ship Moves (Ride the Currents, Hard Burn, Outmaneuver, Brace for Impact, etc.)

Read the Currents or Size It Up

Complications or Escalations as warranted

Travel scenes should be short, sharp, and consequential.
