When you reposition the ship relative to an antagonistic threat—slipping behind a foe, weaving through man-made hazards, breaking pursuit, or lining up an advantage—roll +Poise (reactive flying) or +Insight (anticipatory flying).

10+ — Choose two:

Identify a vulnerable system or behavior pattern.

Break an enemy’s line of fire or establish a clear firing line for your ship.

Create or block off an escape route

Push the enemy into hazards or use terrain to shield your approach.

Break coordination between multiple enemies or stop them from regrouping.

Create a clean approach for boarding, docking, or EVA.

7–9 — Choose one from above; the Keeper introduces a complication tied to the move.

Miss — You misjudge timing or space. The Keeper escalates the situation; mark ship XP.
