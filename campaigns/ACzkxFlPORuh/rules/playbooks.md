Experience, Advancements, and Entanglements

In Haven, experience and advancement should not just be numbers on a page—it’s what the Unbroken Sky remembers about you, and what you choose to carry forward. The more the crew risks, endures, and confronts the shadows of their past, the more they grow into the future.

Mechanically, each crew member has an experience track. When all five boxes are filled, they gain an Advancement from their playbook (or another when allowed), reset their experience to 0, and potentially activate an entanglement.
