A context modifier represents the combined impact of the situation on the action being attempted. A context modifier may reflect:

How difficult or easy the task is in the current fiction

Whether the character’s active Harm or Old Wounds meaningfully interact with the action

Whether relevant gear or gear tags clearly help or hinder the attempt



| Mod | Situation | When to Use It |

| --- | --- | --- |

| –4 | Crushing | The situation is fundamentally hostile to the attempt. Forces at play actively undermine your position, leaving only narrow, costly, or unstable paths forward. |

| –3 | Overmatched | The opposition or environment clearly outclasses you. The situation resists control and punishes mistakes. |

| –2 | Outmatched | Strong disadvantages apply. You are operating from a weakened position, under pressure, or facing superior leverage. |

| –1 | Unfavorable | Conditions introduce friction or risk. The situation complicates the attempt through instability, distraction, or limited control. |

| 0 | Even | The situation offers no meaningful advantage or disadvantage. Circumstances neither help nor hinder the attempt. |

| +1 | Favorable | You have a modest edge. Circumstances support the attempt through preparation, positioning, or timely opportunity. |

| +2 | Advantaged | The situation clearly tilts in your favor. You act with leverage, control, or supportive conditions. |

| +3 | Dominant | You hold overwhelming local control. The situation bends readily to your actions and resists interference. |

| +4 | Overwhelming | The situation is fundamentally aligned with the attempt. Forces at play reinforce your position and amplify your impact. |
