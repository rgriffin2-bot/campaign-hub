# The Absent Engineers
By the time humanity arrived, Haven’s builders were gone. Whether they vanished ten thousand years ago or ten million is argued with equal intensity in labs and temples.

What’s not debated: **Haven works like a designed system**, and someone built it.

## Three Ways the System Talks About Them
Different factions keep different “Makers” in their heads:

- **Gods:** distant originators whose work is a living scripture.
- **Ancestors of mastery:** proof that intelligence can reshape reality.
- **Background miracle:** the sky works because it was made to; don’t touch what you can’t replace.

Most people carry a blend of all three—cynical until they see a miracle up close.

## What They Left Behind
The Maker legacy shows up in two forms:

- **Macro-scale design:** the breathable expanse and its ecological balancing acts.
- **Hidden maintenance:** machines and processes that keep the system “breathing,” often without regard for human convenience.

They also left **caretakers** that still operate: [[lore:stewards]].

## A Useful Rule for Lore
The Makers are best used as *cause, not character*:
- Their absence creates mystery.
- Their artifacts create leverage and danger.
- Their caretakers create consequences.
