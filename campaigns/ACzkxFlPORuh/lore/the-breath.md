# A Living Medium
The Breath is a bio-engineered organism (often described as a nanobiological lattice) that saturates air, water, soil, and flesh throughout Haven. Stay long enough and it colonizes you.

For most people, it’s background miracle:

- longer life
- faster healing
- baseline vitality beyond human ancestry

## The Tilt
For a vanishingly small few, the relationship shifts from “symbiosis” into **attunement**.

These individuals may:

- sense patterns in the Breath
- feel distant presences or anomalies
- influence people, objects, currents, and systems at a distance by manipulating the shared medium

At low levels it looks like intuition. At high levels it looks like storm-shaping, machine-whispering, or telekinesis—effects that force scientists to swear and priests to kneel.
