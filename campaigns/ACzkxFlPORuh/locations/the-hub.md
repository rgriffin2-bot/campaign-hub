The Hub is the political and ceremonial heart of Haven Prime, built around the landing site of the original colony ship *Endeavor*. Wide boulevards radiate outward from the central plaza, lined with government buildings, embassies, and monuments to the colony's founders.

## Key Buildings

### Alliance Council Hall
The seat of government for the Haven Alliance. The grand domed building houses the council chambers, administrative offices, and the Hall of Records.

### The Endeavor Memorial
The preserved remains of the colony ship, now a museum dedicated to the early settlement period. The ship's bridge has been maintained in working condition as a reminder of the journey that brought humanity to this system.

### Embassy Row
A tree-lined avenue hosting diplomatic missions from various factions and independent settlements throughout the system.

## Security

The Hub maintains the highest security presence in the city. Alliance Security officers patrol constantly, and all visitors must pass through checkpoints to enter the district.
