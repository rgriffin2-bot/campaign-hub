Port Tempest is the largest and most influential of Titan's cloudcities, a massive floating platform that serves as the informal capital of Skimmer society. Kept aloft by enormous hydrogen bladders and constant thrust from atmospheric engines, the city rides the eternal storms of the gas giant's upper atmosphere.

## Structure

The cloudcity is built in tiers:

### Upper Decks
Home to the Magister's palace, the captain's council chamber, and the estates of wealthy trading families. The observation domes here offer stunning views of Titan's swirling cloud bands.

### Middle Decks
The working heart of the city - refineries, warehouses, crew quarters, and the famous Sky Market where outsiders are permitted to trade.

### Lower Decks
Engineering sections, hydrogen bladder maintenance, and the docking bays where skimmer ships come and go.

## The Sky Market

The only place where non-Skimmers can legally conduct business on Titan. A sprawling bazaar where traders exchange refined gases, exotic chemicals, and goods from across the system. Strict rules govern behavior here - violence results in immediate expulsion into the clouds below.

## Population

Approximately 8,000 permanent residents, with hundreds more transient traders and crew passing through at any time.
