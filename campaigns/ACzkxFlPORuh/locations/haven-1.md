Haven-1 is the primary star of the Haven System, a stable G-type yellow dwarf that has nurtured life across multiple worlds for billions of years. Its consistent energy output has made this system a prime candidate for colonization.

The star is orbited by three companion stars (Haven-2, Haven-3, and Haven-4), making this a complex quaternary star system. Despite this complexity, the inner habitable zone remains remarkably stable due to the careful orbital mechanics of the system.

## Notable Features

- **Spectral Class**: G2V (similar to Sol)
- **Age**: Approximately 4.8 billion years
- **Luminosity**: 1.02 solar luminosities
- **Mass**: 1.01 solar masses

The star's corona occasionally produces significant solar flares, which the Alliance monitors carefully to protect spacecraft and orbital infrastructure.
