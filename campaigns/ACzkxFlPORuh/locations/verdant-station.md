Verdant Station is the primary orbital facility serving Verdant and the largest space station in the Haven System. Originally constructed as a shipyard, it has evolved into a bustling trade hub and transit point for travelers.

## Facilities

### Docking Bays
The station has 47 docking bays capable of handling everything from personal shuttles to medium freighters. Larger vessels must remain in parking orbit and transfer cargo via shuttle.

### Commerce District
Three levels of shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues cater to spacers and tourists alike. The famous "Starview Lounge" offers panoramic views of Verdant below.

### Shipyard
The original purpose of the station remains its primary industry. The yard can handle repairs and modifications for most civilian vessels, and has contracts with the Alliance Navy for minor maintenance.

## Population

Approximately 125,000 permanent residents, plus a transient population of 20,000-30,000 visitors and workers at any given time.
