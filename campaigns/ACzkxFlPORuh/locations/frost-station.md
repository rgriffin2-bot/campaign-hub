Frost Station is a remote research facility on Frostheim, dedicated to studying the planet's unique geological and atmospheric conditions. The station sits on a stable ice shelf, its heated domes a small island of warmth in the endless frozen landscape.

## Facilities

### Main Dome
The central habitat containing living quarters, dining facilities, and common areas for the research staff. Designed to house up to 50 people comfortably.

### Laboratory Complex
State-of-the-art research facilities including:
- Ice core analysis labs
- Atmospheric monitoring equipment
- Geological survey stations
- A small medical bay

### Vehicle Bay
Heated garage housing the station's fleet of ice crawlers and atmospheric shuttles used for field expeditions.

## Research Focus

The station's primary mission is to study Frostheim's ice sheets, which contain a record of the planet's climate going back millions of years. Secondary projects include atmospheric research and mineral surveys.

## Isolation

Frost Station is one of the most remote inhabited locations in the system. Supply ships visit quarterly, and communication delays make real-time contact with the inner system difficult. The isolation takes a psychological toll, and tours are limited to six months.
