Titan is a massive gas giant in the outer system, its swirling bands of ammonia and methane clouds hiding vast stores of hydrogen and helium. The planet is home to the Skimmers, a unique culture that lives permanently in the planet's upper atmosphere.

## The Skimmers

The Skimmers are a society of atmospheric miners who live in massive floating platforms called "cloudcities." They harvest hydrogen, helium-3, and exotic gases from Titan's atmosphere, trading with the inner system for manufactured goods.

### Culture

Skimmer society is deeply hierarchical, with platform captains holding absolute authority over their vessels and crews. Honor and reputation are paramount, and disputes are often settled through formal duels.

### Technology

Skimmer technology is specialized for life in a gas giant:
- **Cloudcities**: Massive platforms kept aloft by hydrogen buoyancy
- **Skimmer Ships**: Aerodynamic vessels designed for atmospheric flight
- **Extraction Rigs**: Specialized equipment for harvesting gases

## Points of Interest

- **Port Tempest**: Largest cloudcity, serves as the informal capital
- **The Deep**: Legendary region where no skimmer has returned from
- **Sky Market**: Neutral trading post where outsiders can meet Skimmers

## Population

Approximately 35,000 permanent Skimmer residents across dozens of cloudcities.
