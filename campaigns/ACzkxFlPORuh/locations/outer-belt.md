The Outer Belt is a vast ring of asteroids orbiting between Titan and the outer system. Unlike the controlled mining operations on Caldera, the Belt is home to fiercely independent prospectors and small mining cooperatives.

## Politics

The Belt has no central government. Instead, various factions and family claims control different regions:

- **The Free Miners Coalition**: Loose alliance of independent operators
- **Rockhopper Syndicate**: Largest organized faction, controls several major asteroids
- **The Families**: Multi-generational mining clans with hereditary claims

## Economy

Belt miners extract precious metals, rare earth elements, and ice for water and fuel. They trade with both the Alliance and anyone else willing to pay, much to the Mining Guild's frustration.

## Hazards

The Belt is dangerous territory:
- Micro-meteorite strikes
- Claim jumpers and pirates
- Equipment failures far from help
- Radiation exposure

Despite these risks, many prefer the freedom of the Belt to the structured life in the inner system.
