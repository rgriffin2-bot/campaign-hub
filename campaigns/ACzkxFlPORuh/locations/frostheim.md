Frostheim is a frozen world in the mid-system, its surface covered in kilometers-thick ice sheets. Beneath this frozen shell lies a vast liquid water ocean, heated by tidal forces and volcanic activity on the ocean floor.

## The Subsurface Ocean

The discovery of Frostheim's ocean sparked one of the largest scientific expeditions in system history. Drilling stations now punch through the ice to send submersibles into the dark waters below.

### Marine Life

Bioluminescent organisms have been discovered in the deep waters, feeding on chemical vents similar to those found in old Earth's oceans. No complex life has been confirmed, but the search continues.

## Research Installations

- **Deepcore Station**: Primary research facility and drilling operation
- **Ice Harbor**: Surface facility for logistics and personnel
- **Submersible Bay**: Launch and recovery facility for deep-sea exploration vehicles

## Population

Approximately 890,000 researchers, support staff, and contractors. The harsh conditions mean most assignments are temporary, typically 6-12 months.
