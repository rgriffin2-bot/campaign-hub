Caldera is a harsh volcanic world in the inner system, tidally locked to Haven-1. Its surface is a hellscape of molten rivers and toxic clouds, but beneath the crust lies one of the richest deposits of rare earth elements in the sector.

## Mining Operations

The Mining Guild operates seventeen major extraction facilities on Caldera, protected by massive heat shields and atmospheric processors. Workers serve 90-day rotations before mandatory leave, and the pay is among the highest for manual labor in the system.

### Population

Approximately 2.3 million permanent residents live in pressurized habitats, with another 500,000 transient workers cycling through at any given time.

## Points of Interest

- **The Forge**: Central processing facility and administrative hub
- **Shaft Town**: Largest residential district, built into a dormant caldera
- **The Burn**: Notorious cantina district on the terminator line
