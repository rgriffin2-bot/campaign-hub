Verdant is the crown jewel of the Haven System - a successfully terraformed world that serves as the seat of Alliance governance. Originally a barren rock, three centuries of careful atmospheric engineering have transformed it into a lush garden world with breathable air and temperate climates.

## Geography

The planet features three major continents separated by shallow, warm seas. The largest continent, Nova Terra, hosts the capital city of Haven Prime.

### Climate

- **Equatorial regions**: Tropical, heavy rainfall
- **Mid-latitudes**: Temperate, ideal for agriculture
- **Polar regions**: Cold but habitable, popular for winter sports

## Government

Verdant is governed by the Alliance Council, a representative democracy with delegates from each major settlement. The Council Chamber is located in the Spire, a massive arcology in Haven Prime.

## Economy

The planet's economy is diverse:
- Agricultural exports to other system bodies
- Financial services and banking
- Tourism and hospitality
- Government and administration

## Population

45 million permanent residents, making it the most populous world in the system.
