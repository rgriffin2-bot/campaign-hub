Haven's Moon is Verdant's only natural satellite, a small rocky body with no atmosphere. Despite its harsh conditions, a small research colony has been established to study low-gravity agriculture and conduct astronomical observations.

## Research Station Alpha

The primary installation on the moon, Research Station Alpha houses approximately 8,500 scientists, support staff, and their families. The station is largely underground, protected from radiation and micrometeorites.

### Research Programs

- **Low-G Agriculture**: Developing crop strains for use in space habitats
- **Astronomical Observatory**: Taking advantage of the airless environment for deep space observation
- **Medical Research**: Studying long-term effects of reduced gravity on human physiology

## Tourism

Limited tourism is available for visitors who want to experience low gravity. The "Lunar Resort" offers weekend packages including spacewalks and crater tours.
