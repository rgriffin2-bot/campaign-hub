Haven Prime is the largest city on Verdant and serves as the de facto capital of the Haven Alliance. Built around the original colony ship landing site, the city has grown into a sprawling metropolis of over two million inhabitants.

## Districts

### The Hub
The central district built around the preserved hulk of the colony ship *Endeavor*. Government buildings, embassies, and the Alliance Council chambers are located here.

### Portside
The commercial heart of the city, featuring the main spaceport, trading houses, and warehouses. Ships from across the system dock here daily.

### The Terraces
Residential district built into the hillsides overlooking the city. Home to the wealthy and politically connected.

### Lowtown
Working-class neighborhoods spread across the floodplain. Dense, crowded, but with a vibrant street culture.

## Notable Locations

- **Alliance Council Hall**: Where the governing council meets
- **The Endeavor Memorial**: Preserved colony ship turned museum
- **Portside Market**: Largest open-air market in the system
- **Haven University**: Premier educational institution
