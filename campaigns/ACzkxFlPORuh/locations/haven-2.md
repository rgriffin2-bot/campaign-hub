Haven-2 is one of three companion stars orbiting the primary star Haven-1. This smaller orange dwarf maintains a stable orbit and contributes to the unique lighting conditions throughout the system.

Ships traveling through the Haven System must account for the gravitational influence of Haven-2 when plotting courses, particularly when traveling to the outer reaches of the system.
